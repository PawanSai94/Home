Work Experience

Analog Devices

Member of Technical Staff
Aug 2016 - Present

IBIS Modeling: IBIS (I/O Buffer Information Specifications) is a behavioural model that describes the electrical characteristics of the Pins of an IC (Integrated Circuit) These models are used for circuit-level Signal Integrity (SI) analysis. The Timing analysis is performed to overcome any undue SI effects.

IBIS-AMI (Algorithmic Modeling Interface) for full channel simulation, implemented for Serializer-Deserializer (SerDes) designs. Spectre simulations are captured from Analog Design Environment. Python scripts are coded for the ease of workflow. Mentored interns who joined as colleagues later.

An IoT fitness wearable on wrist, that tracks the distance travelled, calories burned, monitors heart rate, maps the unknown GPS terrain with a clock & Temperature sensor. The measurement algorithms were developed by me along with the Schematic design, Coding in Embedded C++ with multi-threading using the Atmega 2560, and designing and fabrication of PCB
